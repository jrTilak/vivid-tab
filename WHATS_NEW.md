# What's New in Vivid Tab 1.4.0

Vivid Tab 1.4 makes the new-tab experience faster, more reliable, and easier to personalize.

## Search faster with bangs

Type a shortcut before your search to go directly to a service—for example, `!yt ambient music`, `!g vivid tab`, or `!gh plasmo`.

## Make it yours

- Choose Dark, Catppuccin Mocha, or Tokyo Night.
- Pick rounded, compact, or square corners.
- Switch between translucent and opaque surfaces.
- Upload wallpapers, bookmark online favorites, and move through cached choices without waiting for another download.

## More reliable widgets

- Quotes are bundled with Vivid Tab and refresh locally on every new tab.
- Notes, to-dos, weather, bookmarks, and layouts have received stability and usability fixes.
- Existing 1.3 preferences are migrated automatically when updating to 1.4.
